How to upload your new students using the Student Upload Page

  1. Go to the 2017-19 Canvas Course Resources Site.
  2. Click the Upload NEW Students tab.
  3. Click the “Click Here to Upload your new Students Button”.
  4. Download the Student Enrollment Request spreadsheet.
  5. Open the Students Enrollment Request spreadsheet and add your students.
  • Do NOT alter the spreadsheet.
  • Do not add additional information.
  • If the student does not have an email address leave the email field empty.
  1. When you've completed your spreadsheet, click Submit Assignment and upload your students' enrollment request file using the space provided.

**ONLY STUDENTS UPLOADED HERE WILL BE PROCESSED.**
